A Quiet Goodbye

Brijesh Valsaraj

← My Writings

When we part on bitter terms, we know,
Apologies will soon follow
Yet parting on good terms doth bring
An end to what once had a ring.

No longer shall we speak or write
Our paths now diverge, lost in the night
Our time together has now passed,
Farewell, adieu, it could not last

And now the shadows close around,
No more the joyous laughter sound
Our memories, like fading flowers, decay,
As we journey alone, day by day
