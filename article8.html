Sweet Refrains of Yesterday

Brijesh Valsaraj

If love or hate, both shall come my way,
I'll take them in stride, come what may,
For in the heart of those who love,
I'll dwell forevermore, like a dove.

And in the mind of those who hate,
I'll linger still, like an unwelcome fare,
For though their thoughts may turn to spite,
I'll shine like a star, in the dark of night

So let them love or let them hate,
I'll stand my ground, and calmly wait,
For in the end, I know my worth,
And in my heart, I'll find my mirth.
