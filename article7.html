In Hearts and Shadows Alike

Brijesh Valsaraj

← My Writings

Sometimes we cling to what causes pain,
For it once brought us joy, like a sweet refrain
Our hearts ache as we hold on tight,
To memories of happiness, now out of sight.

We long for the days when we felt so alive,
when our hearts were light and our spirits would thrive.
But now the clouds have come to stay.
And we struggle to find the light of day.

Still, we hold on, with tears to our eyes,
To the memories that once made us rise
For even in the darkness, they still glow,
And offer us hope, as we navigate the unknown
